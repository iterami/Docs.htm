iterami/Docs.htm/Repos/GMod-Addons.lua
  • The "iterami" addon adds an "iterami" tab to the right side of your spawn menu and to your context menu.
  • Installation:
    1. Subscribe to and enable the Extended Spawnmenu addon.
      • NOTE: "Extended Spawnmenu" is a third-party addon not created or maintained by iterami. Use at your own risk!
      • An issue has been created to remove this requirement.
    2. "git clone https://github.com/iterami/GMod-Addons.lua.git --depth 1"
    3. Copy or link the "iterami" folder into your GMod "addons" folder:
      • Linux: "~/.steam/steam/steamapps/common/GarrysMod/garrysmod/addons/"
      • Mac: ???
      • Windows: ???
    4. Your addons will be reinitialized the next time you join a server.
  • Updating:
    1. "git pull" the "GMod-Addons.lua" repo you cloned.
    2. If you copied instead of linked in installation step #2, copy the "iterami" folder into your "GarrysMod/garrysmod/addons" folder.
    3. Your addons will be reinitialized the next time you join a server.
  • Uninstallation:
    1. Remove the "iterami" folder from your "GarrysMod/garrysmod/addons" folder.
    2. Delete the cloned "GMod-Addons.lua" folder.
    3. Your addons will be reinitialized the next time you join a server.
