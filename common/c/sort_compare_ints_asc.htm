iterami/Docs.htm/common C Projects/sort.c/int sort_compare_ints_asc(const void* a, const void* b)
  • Compares two integers.
  • Used in "qsort()" rather than directly called.
  • Returns a value:
    • 0: a and b are are equal
    • less than 0: a is less than b
    • greater than 0: a is greater than b
Arg Type Notes
a void*
b void*
