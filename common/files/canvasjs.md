### `canvas_attach(args)`
Arg      | Required? | Notes
---------|-----------|-----------------------------------------

entity   | YES       | ID of the entity that is being attached.
offset-x | NO        | Number of pixels of x-axis offset.
offset-y | NO        | Number of pixels of y-axis offset.
