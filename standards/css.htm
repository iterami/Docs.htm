iterami/Docs.htm/Standards/CSS
  • Dark theme colors must be used whenever possible.
  • Each selector should be on its own line with either a comma or an opening brace at the end of the line.
  • Never use color names, only 3-character hex notation.
  • No additional newlines after closing braces, except between base and interaction selector groups.
  • No spaces before opening braces.
  • Put base selectors first and interaction-based selectors separatly at the end of the file, separated by a space.
  • Properties and selectors must be in alphabetical order.
