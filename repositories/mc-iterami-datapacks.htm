iterami/Docs.htm/MC-iterami-DataPacks
  • Installation:
    1. Exit your Minecraft world.
    2. Clone or download the repository.
    3. Copy the directory of the data pack you wish to use into your world save's "datapacks" directory.
  • Updating:
    1. Exit your Minecraft world.
    2. Pull or re-download the repository.
    3. Within your world save's "datapack" directory, delete the directory of the data pack you wish to update.
    4. Copy the directory of the data pack you wish to use into your world save's "datapacks" directory.
  • Uninstallation:
    1. Exit your Minecraft world.
    2. Within your world save's "datapack" directory, delete the directory of the data pack you wish to uninstall.
  • Functions:
    • clear_chunk
    • make_block
    • make_csg
    • make_empty
    • set_default
    • set_peace
Functions Notes
  1. Enable chunk display by pressing "F3 + G".
  2. Go to the northwest corner of the chunk you wish to clear at any Y position.
  3. Run the function to replace all blocks in the chunk with air.
  • If there are no blocks one block below you, this function creates a stone block there.
  • Makes a simple cobblestone generator.
  • Replaces blocks, so be careful where you place it.
  • This function assumes you are playing in an entirely empty world.
  • Creates a bedrock block at 0,100,0 and teleports you on top of it.
  • Returns all "gamerule" commands to their default values.
  • Attempts to make your world as peaceful as possible within "survival" mode:
    • Sets difficulty to "peaceful".
    • Sets weather to "clear".
    • Sets time to "day".
    • Disables raids.
    • Disables fire ticks.
    • Allows you to keep your inventory when you die.
    • Disables mob griefing.
    • Enables natural health regeneration.
