  • Cheats are not used, including limiting knowledge to sensor range.
  • AIs:
    • iterami-competitive: an attempt to create a general-purpose bot that can win competitive games.
    • iterami-cyborgswarm: a bot that swarms enemies with cyborgs that attack every enemy droid they see.
    • iterami-missilefortress: a bot that quickly researches and spams missile fortresses.
  • Installation:
    1. "git clone https://github.com/iterami/WZ2100-AI.js.git --depth 1"
    2. Copy the directories of the bots you wish to use into the "mods/autoload/" directory within your Warzone 2100 directory.
  • Observation:
    1. "shift + backspace" to enable debug and cheats. Keep this mode active for the entire game.
    2. "ctrl + o" and change "Selected Player" to the AI you wish to observe.
    3. You can also attach the AI you wish to observe to whichever player number you are.
    4. Click "Reveal all" to reveal the entire map for easier observation.
    5. "ctrl + +" to increase the game speed for quicker observation.
