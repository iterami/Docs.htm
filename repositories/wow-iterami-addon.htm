  • "WoW-iterami-Addon" alters functionality and adds various slash commands:
    • /iterami_config: loads standardized iterami CVar config.
    • /iterami_print: prints CVars that do not match iterami defaults.
    • Class colors are added to focus and target frames.
    • Colors are added to the name and level of your target and focus: green for friendly units, yellow for neutral units, and red for hostile units.
    • Hide the gryphons that appear on the left and right of the main actionbar.
    • Tooltips are anchored to the cursor rather than being displayed in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  • Installation:
    1. "git clone https://github.com/iterami/WoW-iterami-Addon.git --depth 1"
    2. Copy or link the "iterami" directory into your WoW "Interface/AddOns/" directory:
      • Linux: ???
      • Mac: ???
      • Windows: ???
    3. Your addon will be loaded the next time you start the game.
  • Updating:
    1. "git pull https://github.com/iterami/WoW-iterami-Addon"
    2. If you copied instead of linked in installation step #2, copy the "iterami" directory into your WoW "Interface/AddOns/" directory.
    3. Your addon will be loaded the next time you start the game.
  • Uninstallation:
    1. Remove the "iterami" directory from your WoW "Interface/AddOns/" directory.
    2. Delete the cloned "WoW-iterami-Addon" directory.
    3. Your addon will be unloaded the next time you start the game.
